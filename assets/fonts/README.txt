Font Files Needed:

1. 29LT Bukra Font:
   - 29LTBukra-Regular.ttf (Regular weight)
   - 29LTBukra-Bold.ttf (Bold weight - 700)

2. Aclonica Font:
   - Aclonica-Regular.ttf (Regular weight)

Download Instructions:
- Aclonica: Available on Google Fonts (search for "Aclonica")
- 29LT Bukra: Search online for "29LT Bukra free download" or check:
  * Google Fonts
  * Font Squirrel
  * DaFont
  * Arabic font websites

Place the downloaded .ttf files directly in this fonts/ folder.

After adding the files, run: flutter pub get
